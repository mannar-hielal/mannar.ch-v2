Hi there!

It's Mannar Hielal here!
I was born in Aleppo, worked, studied and lived with my family, until war came and everthing was changed.

In 2017 i came from Syria all the way to Switzerland. back then, all i knew about Switzerland is "oh this dude is rich! he's got a bank account in Switzerland" and "Heidi" the cartoon.

The ladies in my community in Syria say that after 30 a womans goal is to start a family, dedicate herself to a husband, household and bring children, So i did the same (with redefining some variables) i started learning a new language (German), dedicated myself to learn coding and generated few projects :)
